Hi, we are presenting the idea of app called Travlog, application that takes vlogging and travel to next level...

So what is Travlog?

Travlog captures memories works as an informative online series that provides travel video guides for destinations all over the world with an emphasis on activities, accommodation, food, adventure & many more aspects. It is a digital form of blogging comes with the same objective that is to motivate public by sharing the vlogger’s thoughts & showing his/her experiences via short length videos. Basically, it is a platform that promotes destination marketing and encourages people to travel the world by making traveling easy with the maximum use of interactive data.

Problem summary:

Every person has a bucket list to travel the world but there are many complications that stops them from happening. Most of those complications are:
  • They may not have the budget to hire personal travel guide.
  • They may not have the proper detail knowledge and data about the places they want to visit. Existing Apps/maps do not focus on making interactive representation of travel data.
  • There is not any central common platform to get information about hotel prices, tour packages, and travel agencies.
  • They may not be familiar with local languages and culture.
  • Tourists may have problem dealing with currency of particular nation they are visiting.
So, we have integrated all those things required for making travelers experience more fun and easier in one common platform.

To counter the problems travelers are facing we provide different features: Making it as precise as possible:

Target Market:

Our vlogging part is focused on youth and middle age people and who are familiar with editing video technology.

Navigation and destination information is focused on older age groups and people who have little knowledge about mobile applications. We are simplifying our UI and making data interactive to user as much as we can so first-time user of technology can also access our product.

Since our short-term goal is local market in Nepal We will be focusing on those foreign tourists and domestic tourists seeking leisure travel and excursions whilst on vacation in Nepal, with the intention of letting them see and appreciate the numerous attractions in our country. Though we realize that the majority of our tourists come from India and china we shall be mainly targeting those from Europe and the Americas who often do not have transport or adequate knowledge about the country, unlike their South Asia counterparts who often do.

Business Sustainability:

As we know most startups need a certain money to sustain in market and ensure future growth. Our business is also will have certain costs that cannot be avoided if we want to continue our business. At the first phase of business, we will try to collect least cost that can’t be avoided like costs of servers, maintenance costs, etc.

Our revenue stream in our starting phase will not be same when we start growing in the market.  So, we have divided our revenue stream into various phases. Phases are made on the basis of user base that use our application. We will be choosing suitable revenue model as our user base increases.  

For example: at startup, our user base may be low so we have to give our product as a service and collect some kind of donation from users but this is not practical when user base increases as maintenance cost will also increase with user base.  So, while taking off stage of our business, we won’t enforce our user to pay rather we focus on increasing our user base and visibility. After we reach certain predefined user numbers, then we will be changing our revenue stream but we still will not force our users to pay. For example: we may charge money for certain particular service only like hotel booking, ticket booking, reservations, etc. inside our app or add subscription feature for using certain features of our application.
